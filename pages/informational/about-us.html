About Us

Hello, I am Max, the Minifig Tailor!

The creation of the Minifig Tailor storefront came out of a desire to provide custom Lego compatible capes to my friend's and family's creations! There is nothing that makes a minifigure stand out more than some custom threads, and I wanted to offer something accessible to the average builder. I am a builder myself, and every image on the website comes from my personal collection of creations.

I do not charge additional shipping unless I have to use a box to ship. Postage is factored into the price of each item, which allows for simple, afforable orders.

Thank you for checking out the Tailor, and I hope to hear from you soon!
