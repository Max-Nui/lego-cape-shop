Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will my Order ship?

We try and ship out orders within 4 business days of purchase. This time may be increased at times of intense backorder to 7 business days.

Can I use these capes as exact replacement replicas for my figures?

While our products are intended to be compatible with Lego minifigures, it's not a guarantee that the materials/cut will be 1 to 1 accurate with an official product. Often, the tailor has to reconstruct an item's pattern with limited resources, and subtle differences may occur. Please bear this in mind when ordering.
