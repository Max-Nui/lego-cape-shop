Shipping Policy

All orders are processed within 3–5 business days unless otherwise noted. Because each item is carefully prepared and packaged to ensure quality, processing times may vary during high-volume periods. Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and selected delivery method. Once an order has shipped, a confirmation will be provided when available. The Minifig Tailor is not responsible for delays caused by postal carriers, customs processing, or incorrect shipping information provided at checkout. We are not accepting orders from International customers at this time (USA Only).

Certain products are crafted on a made-to-order basis to ensure precision and quality. These items require additional production time before shipment, typically 5–10 business days depending on order volume and complexity. Made-to-order items will ship once production is complete and may extend overall delivery timelines if combined with ready-to-ship products. Because these items are produced specifically for your order, they may not be eligible for cancellation or return once production has begun.
