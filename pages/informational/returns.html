Return Policy

All sales are final. Due to the nature of our products and the small-batch, precision production process, we do not accept returns, exchanges, or issue refunds once an order has been placed.

If your order arrives damaged or you receive an incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with your order number and clear photos of the issue. We will review the claim and, if confirmed, provide a replacement or appropriate resolution at our discretion.

We encourage customers to carefully review product descriptions, color selections, and quantities before completing checkout. If you have questions prior to purchasing, please contact us and we will be happy to assist.
