Privacy Policy

At The Minifig Tailor, the privacy and security of our visitors are paramount. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and how we protect your personal details.

Zero Personal Data Storage on Our Servers:

We do not collect, store, or host any Personally Identifiable Information (PII/PID) such as your full name, physical street address, or full phone number, on our web servers or local site databases.

Secure Payment Processing via PayPal:

All transactions and order checkouts are handled securely off-site through PayPal. PayPal uses advanced encryption protocols (TLS/SSL) and industry-standard security measures to safeguard your transactions.

Local Storage & Shopping Cart Functionality:

Our website uses your browser's Local Storage strictly for essential client-side functions:

To maintain the contents of your custom shopping cart as you browse our catalog.

To preserve your selected product colors, quantities, and items until checkout.

This data remains stored locally on your device's web browser and is not transmitted to or harvested by external marketing servers. Clearing your web browser's cookies or cache will reset your local shopping cart.

Order Lookup & Tracking Data:

When using our Order Lookup feature, we communicate directly with PayPal’s secure API using encrypted access tokens to verify your order status and shipping tracking info. We do not build marketing profiles or track your identity across other websites. We only request transaction details from PayPal necessary to fulfill your order and provide you with shipping updates.

Third-Party Links & LEGO® Disclaimer:

Our website contains navigation links to third-party services (such as postal tracking carriers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.
