About Us

The Minifig Tailor creates precision-cut cloth accessories designed for minifigure-scale characters. Capes and fabric pieces are laser cut for consistent sizing and clean edges, then carefully finished to ensure proper fit, drape, and durability. This shop is built for builders and collectors who value quality over mass production.

Standard designs are available alongside custom commissions, with options for specific fabrics, colors, and cuts to suit individual projects. Every order is handled with care and attention to detail, with the goal of providing reliable, well-made accessories that complement your builds without unnecessary markup or corporate distance.
